I'm excited to develop myself, and share those experiences on this website. I want to make amazing things for amazing people, and work with big names in the industry such as Activision Blizzard, Logitech, Razer or CDPR.

Origin Story

What is "KYODAI"?

Initially when I learnt web development, I was working alone on a website for my family. This was in late 2016, and I barely had any idea what I was doing. I always thought about having my own group of people working together as a team.

Fast forward a couple years and I was talking to my friend about how awesome it would be if we could work together, and that's when "Kishi Kyodai" was first thought of.

"Kyodai" was taken from the Japanese word "Kyuodai" - meaning, "brothers". Meanwhile the word "Kishi" refers to a knight, which we thought just sounded cool, and gave nice continuity to the name. Now whenever I do any work personally, or we work together, we use Kishi Kyodai, or Kyodai, as our brand name.

But that's enough about me...
